About Me

Hi, my name is Chloe Hyde and I am a sports photographer and social media master’s student at the University of Florida.

During my four years as an undergraduate student, I was a student-athlete on the cross country and track teams. During this time I faced numerous injuries and had to take time away from the track. During the spring semester of my sophomore year I joined the University Athletic Association here at the University of Florida and was the first active student-athlete/sports photographer in UF history.

I'm excited to become a Double Gator this May following the completion of my master’s degree accompanied by my previously earned bachelor's in sports and media.
